Echo Deluxe - Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Echo Deluxe, the Discord bot that facilitates communication between servers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our bot. By using Echo Deluxe, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Collection

We collect certain data to provide and improve our services. The data we collect includes:

  • Message IDs: We store Message IDs to enforce moderation actions effectively.
  • Server IDs: Server IDs are used for unique identification purposes and to facilitate server-specific functionality.
  • Channel IDs: Channel IDs are used for unique identification purposes and to facilitate server-specific functionality.
  • User IDs: User IDs are collected for identification purposes and to enforce moderation actions effectively.

2. Data Usage

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: The information is utilized to offer our services, including enabling communication between servers and implementing server-specific settings.
  • Improving User Experience: We analyze the data to enhance our bot's features, user interface, and overall performance.
  • Moderation Purposes: The collected data aids in enforcing appropriate behavior and enforcing moderation actions when necessary.

3. Data Protection

We prioritize the security of your data and implement measures to protect it against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. We store the data on secure servers and restrict access to authorized personnel only.

4. Data Sharing

We may share the collected data with Discord when required or as necessary to comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, or protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

5. Data Deletion

If you wish to have your data removed from our database, you can request its deletion through the Echo Deluxe Support server. Once we verify your identity and the request's legitimacy, we will proceed with the deletion, excluding any data necessary for legal or legitimate business purposes.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any modifications will be communicated through the official support server. Your continued use of Echo Deluxe after the Privacy Policy's changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data or this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our support team through the Echo Deluxe Support server.
